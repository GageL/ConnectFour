Basic Requirements:

● Gameplay should take place in Unity UI.

● Player Input should be mouse-based.

● Play should alternate between an AI Player and a Human player to place a checker into one of the columns.

● AI Player makes random decisions.

● When a checker is dropped into a column, it immediately falls to the lowest available spot.

● Checkers cannot be placed in columns that are full.

● Option to reset the game.

Extra Credit

● Add some basic animations.

● Detect when a game is won.

● Build a simple algorithm that makes optimal decisions to win and employs some strategy.
